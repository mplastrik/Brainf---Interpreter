Brainf**k Interpreter

Welcome to my Brainf**k interpreter! I wrote this in Javascript and used system.css for the retro macOS inspired styling.

Interpreter:

Input

Output

""

Commands:

< Decrement the pointer
> Increment the pointer
- Decrement the byte at the pointer
+ Increment the byte at the pointer
. Output the byte at the pointer
, Input a byte and store it in the byte at the pointer
[ Jump forward past the corresponding ] if the byte at the pointer is zero
] Jump backward to the corresponding [ unless the byte at the pointer is zero
